About Me

Joshua Cruey is a professional photographer based out of Orlando. His work has been published internationally in magazines, newspapers, and websites including The Orlando Sentinel, New York Times, Sports Illustrated, Los Angeles Times, Dance Magazine, US Magazine, USA Today, and many others. Joshua is a versatile photographer with experience in anything from commercial/editorial work to sports photography. He is used to working in a fast paced environment under tight deadlines. Joshua's extensive knowledge in lighting enables him to present extremely dynamic images, always meeting or exceeding his client's expectations. Joshua strives to stay up to date with the latest trends in photography while working to develop innovative methods to keep his photography fresh and exciting.
